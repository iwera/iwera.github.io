Seeing you laugh and not knowing what you are laughing about. Seeing you cry and wanting to cry with you. Seeing someone look at you and being able to feel the weight of their stare. Seeing you over there and wishing you were here with me.

Ships in the night is a series of love letters between artworks floating in a festival. Responding to the aesthetic and conceptual elements of various Next Wave Festival projects, a program of creative texts will be read, published, or hidden alongside each, aiming to create new relationships and weave new conversations across the Festival.

Come along to one or all of the performances or listen to the Ships in the night podcast (here soon), to map your own journey through the festival…
